DAIRY FREE PESTO

DESCRIPTION

Pesto is one is one of my favorite sauces/condiments and can be used on anything from fresh veggies, to pasta, potatoes, and sandwiches!
I love this dairy-free, veggie filled version!

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups fresh arugula
  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • 2 T lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

DIRECTIONS

  1. Chop arugula a bit and add to Vitamix or food processor
  2. Add all other ingredients to Vitamix
  3. Pulse until desired consistency
