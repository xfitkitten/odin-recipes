OATMEAL COOKIES

DESCRIPTION

These cookies have crisp edges and hearty oats. A dash of cinnamon sprinkled on top takes them up a notch.
These can be make with GF flour and vegan butter just substitute 1/1.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) softened unsalted butter
  • 1 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 1/2 cups old fashioned oats
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup toasted hemp seeds
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Beat maple syrup and butter until creamy, add eggs and vanilla and mix thoroughly.
  2. In separate bowl, stir together oats, flour, baking powder, baking soda, spices, salt, coconut, hemp seed, walnuts and raisins.
  3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. Mix until just incorporated.
  4. Chill in refrigerator or several hours.
  5. Drop batter onto cookie sheet using a tablespoon. Flatten for crispy cookies. Sprinkle with cinnamon and/or salt.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes minimum.
